PAYDIRT: DIGGING


Before you can start digging for paydirt, you have a couple of steps to complete.

You will need to purchase yourself a shovel, and some empty sacks!

All equipment you require can be purchased from a worker at the Quarry.

Once you have all the equipment you need, head into the center of the Quarry and dig up your paydirt from the dirt piles within the vicinity.

When you have filled your pockets with as much paydirt as you can carry, you are ready to move onto panning.


To learn about panning, reference the information found here; PAYDIRT: PANNING
PAYDIRT: PANNING


Now that you have collected some paydirt, you are ready to start panning!

You will need a gold pan. These can be purchased from the Quarry worker.

Once you have your gold pan and paydirt, head to any body of water and begin panning!

If you are lucky and you manage to find yourself a few precious metal nuggets, you can take a journey to the Foundry and smelt your nuggets into something a little more saught after!

If you dont manage to find any metals, head back to the Quarry and fill up some more sacks!


To learn about smelting, reference the information found here; FOUNDRY: SMELTING
QUARRY: BLASTING


Before you can start blasting rocks, you need to purchase some equipment!

You will need a mining permit, jackhammer and some sticks of dynamite. These can be purchased from the Quarry worker.

Once you have all of your equipment look for the blasting markers around the Quarry walls, our surveyors have marked some safe locations for you!

Once you have found a marker, place your dynamite and move to a safe distance!

Your dynamite should losen up some rocks for you to drill. You can drill the rocks with your jackhammer to gain ores or gems!


To learn about smelting, reference the information found here; FOUNDRY: SMELTING
QUARRY: CAVING


Caving works the same as blasting except it is higher risk, higher reward!

You will need a caving permit, jackhammer and some sticks of dynamite. These can be purchased from the Quarry worker.

Once you have all of your equipment look for the blasting markers inside the Quarry cave system, our surveyors have marked some safe locations for you!

Once you have found a marker, place your dynamite and move to a safe distance!

Your dynamite should losen up some rocks for you to drill. You can drill the rocks with your jackhammer to gain ores or gems!

QUARRY: SMELTING


Now that you have collected a healthy amount of ores, you are ready to smelt them into something more profitable!

Head inside the Quarry cave system and deposit your materials into the furnace, to smelt your ores into metal ingots. You will need your caving permit to get inside.

This will take a short while so make sure to come with patience.

Once you have finished smelting you are ready to sell your ingots to the Builders Warehouse!


To learn about selliing, reference the information found here; SELLING: WAREHOUSE

SELLING: WAREHOUSE


When you are ready to earn some money for all that hard work, head over to the Builders Warehouse!

The Builders Warehouse will purchase any of your smelted ingots for a reasonable price. Just speak to the warehouse worker and they will help you out.

You may be able to purchase back some ingots if you need them for some home projects, depending on the warehouses current stock count.
SELLING: JEWELLER


Hopefully you were saving any gemstones you managed to find whilst panning or mining because the Jeweller is always buying!

The Jeweller will purchase any of your gemstones for a reasonable price. Just speak to the jeweller and they will help you out.

You may be able to purchase back some gemstones if you need them for your crafting projects, depending on the jewellers current stock count.
